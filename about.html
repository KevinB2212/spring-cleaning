🧹 Spring Cleaning

Democratic house accountability, powered by photos & votes

v1.5.0 • 100% Daily Active • Real-World Solution

⚠️ The Problem

Five housemates. One increasingly dirty house. The old system?

  • ❌ Messy: Accusations scribbled on a whiteboard
  • ❌ Inaccurate: People disagreed on what counted as "dirty"
  • ❌ Subjective: No proof, just arguments
  • ❌ Unfair: Same person always blamed
  • ❌ Untrustworthy: Tallies were erased and "disputed"

Result: Arguments, resentment, and a house that got progressively worse.

💡 The Solution

Spring Cleaning replaces subjective arguments with objective democracy.

🎯 How it works:
1. Post a photo of the problem area
2. 4 housemates vote (Guilty or Not Guilty)
3. Majority wins (3+ votes = guilty)
4. Points accumulate (3 points = punishment)
5. Real-time leaderboard shows who's behind

Why It Works

Photo proof removes subjectivity. Nobody can argue "I didn't see it" or "it wasn't that bad" when there's evidence.

Democratic voting ensures fairness. You need 3/4 votes, so one person's vendetta doesn't stick.

Real-time accountability changes behavior. Everyone sees the leaderboard. It matters.

✨ Features

📸 Post Accusations with Photo Proof

Upload a photo, describe the issue, timestamp it. No vague complaints.

🗳️ Democratic Voting

Other 4 housemates vote. Need 3+ to convict. Can't vote on your own case (fairness rules).

🛡️ Appeal System

Accused? Submit a written defense before voting closes. Your housemates can reconsider.

📊 Real-Time Leaderboard

Live points tracker. See who has the most violations. Streaks for clean periods.

📱 Mobile-First PWA

Install on your phone. Works offline. Push notifications for votes & verdicts.

🔐 Secure & Private

Firebase Auth. Each housemate has their own login. Photos stored privately.

⏰ Auto-Timestamped

Every accusation is timestamped (UTC). Impossible to dispute when it happened.

🗳️ Vote Audit Trail

See who voted for what (transparent). No hidden votes changing outcomes.

📈 History & Stats

Browse all past accusations, appeals, and verdicts. Data never disappears.

🎮 Gamification

Streaks for clean periods. Achievements for staying guilt-free. Make it fun.

🌙 Dark Mode

Sleek dark UI. Easy on the eyes. Looks professional, not like a homework project.

⚡ Real-Time Updates

Everyone sees votes & results instantly. No waiting, no delays.
